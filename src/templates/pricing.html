Frequently Asked Questions

How does the assessment counting work?
Each API call to our assessment endpoint counts as one assessment. This includes both student answer evaluations and question validations. Unused assessments don't roll over to the next month.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
If you exceed your monthly assessment limit, additional assessments are charged at $0.01 each for Starter, $0.005 for Professional, and $0.003 for Business plans.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan. You can start using the API immediately after registration with your free Starter plan.
